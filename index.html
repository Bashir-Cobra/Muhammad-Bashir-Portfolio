Case Study — Ourvita

Industry: Cosmetics

Ourvita is a premium natural cosmetics brand delivering high-performance skincare and beauty products powered by nature — built on the idea that science and botanicals work best in harmony, for eco-conscious women and men aged 18–45.

  • Wordmark logo system
  • Nature-inspired, botanical positioning
  • Eco-friendly & toxin-free messaging
  • Premium & safe product line

The wordmark hides a leaf in its negative space — a nod to Ourvita's toxin-free, nature-first commitment — set in a soft, rounded sans-serif that reads as clean, modern and safe. Green was chosen deliberately: it speaks to nature, health, vitality and growth all at once.

Full identity rollout: stationery, product packaging, app and website mockups, all carrying the same wordmark, palette and typeface.

Poppy — App Design

Client: Poppy — Premium Movie & TV Streaming App

Poppy is a subscription-based movie and TV streaming app designed to deliver a seamless viewing experience across mobile, tablet and smart TV — with personalised recommendations, curated collections and an intuitive interface.

  • Minimalist, cinematic, immersive dark UI
  • Personalised home screen with trending content
  • Search & filter by genre, actor and more
  • Watchlist and continue-watching sections
  • Video player with subtitles & playback controls
  • Subscription management & in-app purchases

Target audience: ages 16–45, globally distributed — the whole flow from sign-up to checkout was mapped and designed screen by screen.
